Not taking

Laptop

A laptop is required for collecting notes from lectures. your laptop doesnt need to be a fast one, or a gaming one with all the flashy lighs or the bells and whistles. just a simple laptop to take notes, or you could use an iPad or tablet, hey even use your phone.

Note taking

Notbook and pen

If ou down have a laptop never fear, a trusty pen and notebook is just as good at taking notes. it might be a little oldschool but a good notbook can help you organise all of your work into class sections. Keep on top of it.
